About NISAB 3.0

NISAB 2.0 is a student-focused training initiative organized by NIFES to help young people develop practical and profitable skills beyond the classroom.

Participants will learn directly from experienced instructors through projects, workshops, assignments, and live practical sessions.

Available Skills Track

Frontend Web Development

Learn HTML, CSS, JavaScript, responsive design, and modern web development practices.

Graphics Design

Master branding, social media design, typography, posters, and visual communication.

Video Editing

Learn cinematic editing, transitions, storytelling, and motion graphic.

UI/UX Design

Understand user experience, wireframing, prototyping, and interface design.

Mobile App Development

Build Android applications and cross-platform apps using modern tools.

Digital Marketing

Learn social media growth, content marketing, and audience targeting.
